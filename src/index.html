ABOUT

We're here to take the stress out of making an impact. As an end-to-end development agency, specialised in web-based services, Enkosi is all you need to implement and optimize your great ideas!

Development

From apps and microservices to databases and deployment pipelines, we provide solutions that will take you from mockup to scalable service

Design

Good solutions require good design, and we prioritize delightful user experience as highly as robust application architecture

Analytics

You are what you measure, and from telemetry that minimizes downtime to usage monitoring that reveals user behaviour, we'll provide the best data to help you make the right decisions

Marketing

Building is only half the battle, so we'll help you leverage online ads and social media to bring your target audience to you

HOW CAN WE HELP?

We're able to join you at whatever stage along the development process you find yourself.

Our initial planning and evaluation consultations can help you flesh out the technical requirements, user interface, and market considerations for a potential idea, resulting in a development pack that includes system diagrams, wireframe mockups, and feasibility study information.

From here, we can take you through to a prototype stage using an agile and collaborative approach, helping you explore options and possibilities as your idea is transformed into usable software.

We are also able to continue through to a full implementation of a productionised solution, including thorough testing and a robust, maintainable deployment pipeline.

Alongside this, we are able to both set up and maintain analytical services to ensure you have comprehensive reporting about the performance of your product.
